Step 2: Optimize Your Prompts

❌ Bad Prompt

"What do you think about this DeFi protocol?"

✅ Good Prompt

"Analyze Aave V3's risk parameters, TVL trends, and governance proposals from the last 30 days. Focus on liquidation risks and yield sustainability."

Step 3: Advanced Techniques

Chain-of-Thought Prompting
Ask the AI to "think step by step" for complex analysis tasks
Role-Based Prompts
"Act as a DeFi researcher analyzing..." gets more specialized responses
Context Loading
Provide relevant docs, charts, or data before asking questions
Multi-Agent Workflows
Use different agents for research → analysis → execution
